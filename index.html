The UPRI Internship Program offers students from diverse academic backgrounds, both within and beyond the University, a valuable opportunity to apply the theories and techniques learned in class to real-world challenges in Disaster Risk Reduction and Management (DRRM). Students also benefit from the chance to learn directly from professionals in the field.
Through this program, the UP Resilience Institute (UPRI) reinforces its commitment to strengthening the academic community’s role in integrating and mainstreaming DRRM and Climate Change Adaptation and Mitigation (CCAM) into education, empowering learners to become more resilient to climate and disaster risks.

Undergraduate Internship Program

First launched in 2020 during the midyear term of the UP School Calendar, this program has been open to undergraduates each midyear since then.
The UPRI welcomes undergraduate interns from various UP constituent universities as well as from numerous higher education institutions across the country. Initially conducted online due to the pandemic, the program transitioned to in-person participation starting in 2022.
UPRI’s commitment to a multidisciplinary approach in DRRM and CCAM allows it to host students from a wide array of academic backgrounds.
